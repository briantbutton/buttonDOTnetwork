How Does It Work?


ButtonNet puts a web server right in the building with you.  The result is fast sharing for people under the same roof, no matter where that roof is.

Content stays in the building, so your backhaul Internet speed is not important.  Your guests may share, label and organize videos and pictures, all at 'local speed'.

Local speed is just about instantaneous -- you need to see it to believe it.
